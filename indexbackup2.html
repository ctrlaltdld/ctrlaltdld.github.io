    • Writing concise workflows and tutorials for newcomers to the GeMS schema, both within SCGS and nationwide.
  • Devises enhancements for field workflows involving ArcGIS Survey123 and ArcGIS Field Maps in both Piedmont and Coastal Plain mapping environments.

  • Responsible for distribution of GIS data to the public.
